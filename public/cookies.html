Cookies Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and actions. StreamUp uses minimal cookies—only what is necessary for the website to function properly.

2. Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies Only

StreamUp uses only essential cookies for core website functionality:

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
language Local Storage Remembers your language preference (BR, EN, ES) Until manually cleared
cookie_consent Local Storage Stores your cookie preference choice 1 year

No personal data is stored in these cookies. They are purely functional and do not track your behavior.

3. What We Do NOT Use

  • ✗ Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • ✗ Tracking pixels or tags
  • ✗ Marketing or advertising cookies
  • ✗ Third-party profiling or retargeting

We do not profile, track, or monitor your behavior beyond providing the service you request.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website links to external services that may use their own cookies:

  • Telegram Privacy Policy
  • Telegram Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts (minimal tracking)

We do not control these third-party cookies. Review their policies if you have concerns.

5. Managing Cookies

Clear Cookies from StreamUp

To delete StreamUp's local storage cookies:

  1. Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12 on most browsers)
  2. Go to Application → Local Storage
  3. Find and clear entries for StreamUp's domain
  4. The cookie consent banner will reappear on next visit

Browser-Level Cookie Management

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality (e.g., language selection won't persist).
