Privacy Policy

1. Data Controller

StreamUp IPTV — Premium IPTV subscription service worldwide.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data, only what is strictly necessary to provide and support the IPTV service:

  • Contact information — Telegram number or Telegram handle provided voluntarily when requesting support or ordering.

We do NOT collect: Email addresses, real names, payment card details, location data, browsing history, or any sensitive personal information.

  • Legitimate interest — service security, abuse prevention, and technical support.

4. Data Retention

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for any purpose. Data is shared only when:

  • Required by law or court order.
  • Necessary to prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats.

Your data may be processed and stored in multiple jurisdictions to ensure service availability and redundancy. We ensure compliance with applicable data protection laws in all locations.
